Q: "How do you use this app in the classroom?"

A: Navigate to the S&T page and shake your mobile. This triggers the cards to flip and present a project idea to do and a genre / medium to present it as.

Q: "What if I don't understand the do or as?"

A: Click on the card to find out more on the web.

About

Shake & Teach

is a support tool for teachers:

  • use it for lesson starters, review of prior learning, or performance assessment activities.
  • or in those moments when we all get stuck for a fresh idea or a new approach during a lesson.

Instead of the "just write about it" inertia, you are now just a shake away from thousands of possible activities.

When shaken (or clicked) this app will generate 100,000 unique learning challenge teaching ideas.
